> [!NOTE] 解题思路
> 对于分段填充定义的函数，我们通常用定义求偏导数。$f_x(0,0)=\lim\limits_{h\to 0}\frac{f(h,0)-f(0,0)}{h}$=0

> [!NOTE] 解题思路
> 连续性用极坐表换元易得
> 用定义求偏导可得，在$0^+$处导数为1，$0^-$处则为-1，所以偏导数不存在

> [!NOTE] 第五题
>  $f(x,y) = \begin{cases}xysin\frac{1}{x^2+y^2}& \text{如果 } x^2+y^2\neq 0 \\ 0 & \text{如果 } x^2+y^2 =0 \end{cases}$，讨论函数在(0,0)处的可微性


> [!NOTE] 解题思路
> 若$|\frac{\Delta f(x,y)-A\Delta x-B\Delta y}{\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}}|=0$,$(\Delta y,\Delta x)趋近于(0,0)$则代表在（0，0）处可微。其中这里的A,B都为其在(0,0)处的偏导。易得其在(0,0)处的偏导均为0。所以判别式变为$|\frac{\Delta x \Delta y sin(\frac{1}{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2 })}{\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}}|=0$,易得此式趋近于0，所以在此处可微。

> [!NOTE] 补充
> 可微性判别式的来源，首先微分是指把函数的变化变为其变量的线性表示，考察的是函数在极小领域内是否有线性性。所以若可微则有$\Delta f(x,y)=A\Delta x+B\Delta y+o(\Delta\rho)$(这里移项判定是否右边为$o(\Delta\rho)$即可)

> $设z = \arctan(xy)，y = e^{x}，求\frac{dz}{dx}$

> 3.t和x，y有关，不再赘述
> 4.x,y和u，v有关，要考虑两个偏导
> 5.把x+y看作u，xy看作v，同理
> 6.与5同理

> [!NOTE] 链式法则理念

> [!NOTE] 第一题
> 求下列函数的高阶偏导数
$z = x^{4}+y^{4}-4x^{2}y^{2}$，所有二阶偏导数；
$z = e^{x}(\cos y + x\sin y)$，所有二阶偏导数；
$z = x\ln(xy)，\frac{\partial^{3}z}{\partial x^{2}\partial y}，\frac{\partial^{3}z}{\partial x\partial y^{2}}$；
$u = xyze^{x + y+z}，\frac{\partial^{p + q + r}u}{\partial x^{p}\partial y^{q}\partial z^{r}}$；

$u = f(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})$，所有二阶偏导数；

> [!NOTE] 解答
> 1.2.3都慢慢求导就行了，我们直接讨论第四题，对于乘积函数的不定次阶导，我们可以使用[莱布尼茨公式](Leibniz求导公式.md)（详见[扩展一](#扩展一)）
> 即$\frac{\partial^{n}(fg)}{\partial x^{n}}=\sum_{k = 0}^{n}\binom{n}{k}\frac{\partial^{k}f}{\partial x^{k}}\frac{\partial^{n - k}g}{\partial x^{n - k}}$
> p带入得到$\frac{\partial u^p}{\partial x^p}=(xyz+pyz)e^{x+y+z}$(p大于等于1)
> 同理对y用莱布尼茨公式得$\frac{\partial u^{p+q}}{\partial x^py^q}$=$(xyz+qxz+pyz+pqz)e^{x+y+z}(q大于等于1)$
> 同理对z使用得$\frac{\partial u^{p+q+r}}{\partial x^py^qz^r}$=$(xyz+qxz+pyz+pqz+rxy+rqx+rpy+rpq)e^{x+y+z}(r\geq 1)$对于各个等于0的情况，验证也是满足的
> 接下来的题目换元后，把握好变量之间的联系即可

> [!NOTE] 第二题
> 设u=f(x,y)，x=rcos$\theta$,y=rsin$\theta$，证明：$\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial r^{2}}+\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^{2}}\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial\theta^{2}}=\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x^{2}}+\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial y^{2}}$

> [!NOTE] 解答
> $\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}$=$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial r}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial r}$=$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos\theta+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\sin\theta$
> $\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta}$=$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial \theta}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial \theta}$=$-\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}r\sin\theta+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}r\cos\theta$
> $\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial r^{2}}=\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}\right)=\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos\theta+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\sin\theta\right)$
> $\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial r^{2}} = \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x^{2}}(\cos\theta)^{2}+ 2\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x\partial y}\cos\theta\sin\theta + \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial y^{2}}(\sin\theta)^{2}$
> $\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial \theta^{2}}=\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta}\right)=\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left(-\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}r\sin\theta+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}r\cos\theta\right)$
> $\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial \theta^{2}}=-r^{2}\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x^{2}}(\sin\theta)^{2}-r^{2}\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial y^{2}}(\cos\theta)^{2}$
> $\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{1}{r}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos\theta+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\sin\theta\right)$
> 加起来发现等于右式，得证

> [!NOTE] 第三题
> 设u=f(r),r=${x_1}^2+{x_2}^2+···+{x_n}^2$，证明：$\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x_{1}^{2}}+\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x_{2}^{2}}+\cdots+\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x_{n}^{2}}=\frac{d^{2}u}{dr^{2}}+\frac{n - 1}{r}\frac{du}{dr}$

> [!NOTE] 解答
> 首先形如r和分量x的偏导都等于x/r，所以$\frac{\partial r}{\partial x_i}=\frac{x_i}{r}$,$\frac{\partial^{2}r}{\partial x_{i}^{2}}=\frac{1}{r}-\frac{{x_i}^2}{r^3}$
> $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x_i}=\frac{du}{dr}\frac{x_i}{r}$,$\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x_{i}^{2}}=\frac{d^2u}{dr^2}\frac{{x_i}^2}{r^2}+\frac{du}{dr}(\frac{1}{r}-\frac{{x_i}^2}{r^3})$,所以累加后等于右边

> [!NOTE] 第五题
> 证明：若函数f在区域D上存在偏导数，且$f_{x}=f_{y} \equiv 0$，则f在区域上为常量函数。

> [!NOTE] 思路
> 和单变量的思路一样，使用二元函数中值定理。设区域上任意一点(a,b)有，f(a,b)=C。根据中值定理有$f(a+h,b+k)-f(a,b)=f_x(a+\theta h,b+\theta k)h+f_y(a+\theta h,b+\theta k)k$，因为偏导都为0,所以两点相等。因为h和k的任意性，所以有$f(x,y)\equiv f(a,b)=C$

> [!NOTE] 第四题
> $设函数((x,y)具有连续的n阶偏导数，试证函数g(t)=f(a + ht,b + kt)的n阶导数$
> $\frac{\mathrm{d}^{n}g(t)}{\mathrm{d}t^{n}}=\left(h\frac{\partial}{\partial x}+k\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\right)^{n}f(a + ht,b + kt)(这个东西在书本泰勒展开处给过定义)$

> [!NOTE] 解答
> 证明过程：
> n=1时 根据复合函数求导的链式法则： 令$x = a + ht，y = b + kt，则 g'(t)$= $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \cdot \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \cdot \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}.$ 由于$\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t} = h，\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t} = k$，代入得:$g'(t) = h \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right) f(a + ht, b + kt).$ 因此，当n=1时，命题成立。
> 假设当$n = m,m \geq 1$时命题成立，即:$\frac{\mathrm{d}^{m}g(t)}{\mathrm{d}t^{m}} = \left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{m} f(a + ht, b + kt).$ $其中，\left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{m} 是一个微分算子$，表示对函数\(f\)连续应用\(m\)次偏导数运算，展开为： $\left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{m}$ = $\sum_{i=0}^{m} \binom{m}{i} h^{i} k^{m-i} \frac{\partial^{m}}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i}}$.
>  *证明n = m+1时成立,对归纳假设两边关于t求导： $\frac{\mathrm{d}^{m+1}g(t)}{\mathrm{d}t^{m+1}} = \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} \left[ \left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{m} f(a + ht, b + kt) \right].$ 根据复合函数求导法则，上式右边的每一项$\frac{\partial^{m} f}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i}}$ 对t求导时，需再次应用链式法则：$\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} \left( \frac{\partial^{m} f}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i}} \right) = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \frac{\partial^{m} f}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i}} \right) \cdot \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t} + \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \left( \frac{\partial^{m} f}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i}} \right) \cdot \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}.$ 由于$\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t} = h，(\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t} = k$，代入得：$\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} \left( \frac{\partial^{m} f}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i}} \right) = h \frac{\partial^{m+1} f}{\partial x^{i+1} \partial y^{m-i}} + k \frac{\partial^{m+1} f}{\partial x^{i} \partial y^{m-i+1}}.$ 将此结果代入原式，并整理得： $\frac{\mathrm{d}^{m+1}g(t)}{\mathrm{d}t^{m+1}} = \left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right) \left( \left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{m} f \right)$ = $\left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{m+1} f(a + ht, b + kt).$  因此，当n = m+1时，命题也成立。 由数学归纳法可知，对于任意正整数n，命题成立，即：$\frac{\mathrm{d}^{n}g(t)}{\mathrm{d}t^{n}} = \left( h \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + k \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)^{n} f(a + ht, b + kt).$

> [!NOTE] 第八题
> $设f在点P_0(x_0,y_0)可微，且在P_0给定了n个向量\boldsymbol{l}_i，i = 1,2,\cdots,n$，$相邻两个向量之间的夹角为\frac{2\pi}{n}。证明\sum_{i = 1}^{n}f_{\boldsymbol{l}_i}(P_0)=0$

> [!NOTE] 解答1
> 证明过程方向导数公式
> $因为f在点P_0(x_0,y_0)可微，对于单位向量\boldsymbol{l}_i = (\cos\alpha_i,\cos\beta_i)$，根据方向导数的定义，$f在点P_0沿\boldsymbol{l}_i方向的方向导数f_{\boldsymbol{l}_i}(P_0)=f_x(P_0)\cos\alpha_i + f_y(P_0)\cos\beta_i$。 $那么\sum_{i = 1}^{n}f_{\boldsymbol{l}_i}(P_0)=f_x(P_0)\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\alpha_i + f_y(P_0)\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\beta_i$ 
> 设$\omega = e^{i\frac{2\pi}{n}}=\cos\frac{2\pi}{n}+i\sin\frac{2\pi}{n}$，
> 考虑复数形式的单位向量。 若第一个向量$\boldsymbol{l}_1对应的复数为z_1 = 1（即\cos0 + i\sin0 ），那么\boldsymbol{l}_i对应的复数z_i=\omega^{i - 1}=\cos\frac{2\pi(i - 1)}{n}+i\sin\frac{2\pi(i - 1)}{n}$。$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\frac{2\pi(i - 1)}{n}$是复数$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\omega^{i - 1}$的实部，$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\sin\frac{2\pi(i - 1)}{n}$是复数$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\omega^{i - 1}$的虚部。 根据等比数列求和公式$S_n=\frac{a(1 - q^n)}{1 - q}$（其中a = 1，$q=\omega$，$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\omega^{i - 1}=\frac{1\times(1 - \omega^n)}{1 - \omega}$。 由于$\omega^n = e^{i\frac{2\pi}{n}\times n}=e^{i2\pi}=1$，所以$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\omega^{i - 1}=\frac{1 - 1}{1 - \omega}=0$。 这就意味着$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\frac{2\pi(i - 1)}{n}=0且\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\sin\frac{2\pi(i - 1)}{n}=0$ 。 回到$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}f_{\boldsymbol{l}_i}(P_0)=f_x(P_0)\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\alpha_i + f_y(P_0)\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\beta_i$，因为$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\alpha_i = 0$，$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}\cos\beta_i = 0$，所以$\sum_{i = 1}^{n}f_{\boldsymbol{l}_i}(P_0)=0$ 。 综上，命题得证。

> [!NOTE] 解答2
> $设单位向量\boldsymbol{l}_i = (\cos\theta_i,\sin\theta_i)，i = 1,2,\cdots,n$，已知相邻两个向量之间的夹角为$\frac{2\pi}{n}$，则$\theta_{i + 1}-\theta_i=\frac{2\pi}{n}$（这里$\theta_{n+1}=\theta_1 + 2\pi$ 。

# 扩展二

> [!NOTE] 多元函数莱布尼兹求导公式
> 设 $u = u(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_m)和v = v(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_m)$为多元函数，则它们乘积的高阶混合偏导数可以表示为： $\frac{\partial^{|\alpha|}(uv)}{\partial x_1^{\alpha_1}\partial x_2^{\alpha_2}\cdots\partial x_m^{\alpha_m}} = \sum_{\substack{\beta_1 + \gamma_1 = \alpha_1 \\ \beta_2 + \gamma_2 = \alpha_2 \\ \vdots \\ \beta_m + \gamma_m = \alpha_m}} \binom{\alpha}{\beta} \left( \frac{\partial^{|\beta|}u}{\partial x_1^{\beta_1}\partial x_2^{\beta_2}\cdots\partial x_m^{\beta_m}} \right) \left( \frac{\partial^{|\gamma|}v}{\partial x_1^{\gamma_1}\partial x_2^{\gamma_2}\cdots\partial x_m^{\gamma_m}} \right)$
> 其中$\alpha = (\alpha_1, \alpha_2, \ldots, \alpha_m)$是多重指标，$|\alpha| = \alpha_1 + \alpha_2 + \cdots + \alpha_m$表示偏导数的总阶数。$\beta = (\beta_1, \beta_2, \ldots, \beta_m)$和 $\gamma = (\gamma_1, \gamma_2, \ldots, \gamma_m)$也是多重指标，满足$\beta_i + \gamma_i = \alpha_i$对所有$i = 1, 2, \ldots, m$成立。
