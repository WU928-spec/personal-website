则有 $y=X^T\beta$
设$Y=(1,x_1)$
$X=\begin{pmatrix} 1 & x_{11} & \cdots & x_{1p} \\ 1 & x_{21} & \cdots & x_{2p} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 1 & x_{m1} & \cdots & x_{mp} \end{pmatrix}$

$Y=\begin{pmatrix}y_1\\y_2\\\vdots\\y_m  \end{pmatrix}$

$Y=X\beta^T$

$\hat{\beta} = (X^T X)^{-1} X^T Y$
